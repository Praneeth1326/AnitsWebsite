Admission Procedure - B.Tech Programme
The EAPCET - Engineering, Agriculture and Pharmacy Common Entrance Test convener fills up 70% (Category - A) of the seats by allotting the candidates to this institute on the basis of their choice, their EAPCET rank and the reservation policy of the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh.The remaining 30% (Category - B) of the seats are filled up by the Institute under the Management /NRI quota as per the guidelines given by the APSCHE and the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh for this purpose. The convener of ECET admits 10% of the sanctioned intake for each program for admission directly into the II year of the 4-year B.Tech program from among the Diploma holders, based on their rank in ECET, their choice of the institute and the reservation policy of the Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, under the lateral entry scheme.The following is the sanctioned intake as per AICTE file No: F.No. South-Central/1-43656147326/2024/EOA/Corrigendum-1 Date: 22-May-2024 UG Sanctioned Intake:

Post Graduate

M.Tech - Eligibility Requirements:

A bachelor's degree in Engg/Technology of recognized University in the concerned discipline. AMIE/AMIETE as equivalent to B.Tech. valid GATE Score obtained in the GATE Examination. Apart from requirements of qualifications as stated above, the admissions of sponsored candidates shall be taken. The number of Sponsored candidates shall not be more than two in each specialization and not more than five in each department. These seats are considered as supernumerary. The sponsoring agency shall-certify that the candidates will be granted leave for pursuing the M.Tech. Full-Time course. The candidates should satisfy local/non-local status requirement as laid down in the AP Educational institutions (Regulation of Admissions) Order 1974 as amended from time to time.
