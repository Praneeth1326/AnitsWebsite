DEPARTMENT OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERING

The Mechanical Engineering Department at Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology and Sciences (ANITS) was established in the academic year 2005-06. It began with a B.Tech intake of 60 and has since grown to a B.Tech intake of 240 and an M.Tech (Machine Design) intake of 18. The department is autonomous and NBA accredited. Here's a more detailed look at the department's history and development: Founding: The department was established in 2005-06, initially offering a B.Tech program with a modest intake. Growth: Since its inception, the department has experienced significant growth in terms of student intake and infrastructure development. Program Offerings: The department currently offers a B.Tech program with an intake of 240 and an M.Tech (Machine Design) program with an intake of 18
